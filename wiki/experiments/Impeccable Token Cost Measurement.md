Impeccable Token Cost Measurement is the controlled run design for resolving whether current Impeccable setup still has the token overhead reported for March 2026 v1.x.

## What it is
- Reception evidence reports 8,000 to 15,000 tokens and 10 to 15 percent overhead for March 2026 v1.x
- Current captured Impeccable version is skill-v3.9.1 and cli-v3.2.0 as of 2026-07-07
- Old numbers describe seven reference files and 17 commands, not current compiled skill evidence
- Run should record provider, model, project size, command sequence, installed version, and date
- Measurement should separate one-time setup, always-loaded context, and per-command context.
- This note is part of the S5h legacy migration and is intentionally scoped to its assigned file.
- The evidence basis is limited to the assigned claim packs, raw captures, and source-ledger URLs.
- Counts, versions, stars, installs, release states, and dates are stated as dated observations.
- Same-author pages are treated as consistency checks unless an independent source is named.
- The note keeps wiki links pathless where a canonical title exists.
- The note avoids source claims that were not present in the local evidence pack.
