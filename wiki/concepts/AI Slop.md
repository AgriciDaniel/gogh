AI slop is the default cluster of same-looking frontend choices produced when a model samples from common training-data patterns instead of a project-specific design read.

## What it is
- Theory pack defines frontend AI slop as distributional convergence
- Common signature includes purple-to-blue gradients, Inter-like neutral type, rounded cards, centered heroes, and generic feature grids
- Anthropic names distributional convergence as the frontend-design launch problem
- prg.sh explains the purple default as training-data and tutorial saturation
- Taste Skill answers slop with forbidden patterns, dials, locks, and pre-flight
- Impeccable answers the same pressure with deterministic detector rules
- The six-skill stack treats slop as an operating problem rather than a vague complaint.
- This note is part of the S5h legacy migration and is intentionally scoped to its assigned file.
- The evidence basis is limited to the assigned claim packs, raw captures, and source-ledger URLs.
- Counts, versions, stars, installs, release states, and dates are stated as dated observations.
- Same-author pages are treated as consistency checks unless an independent source is named.
