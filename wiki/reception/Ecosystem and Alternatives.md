Ecosystem and Alternatives maps the six covered skills into a broader design-skill market of curation lists, directories, variants, and adjacent tools.

## What it is
- wilwaldon toolkit curates 70 or more tools across design skills, theming, animation, UI intelligence, design-to-code, testing, docs, framework skills, deploy, and stacks
- Ecosystem pack records a layered mechanism map across the six covered skills
- Anthropic is baseline taste prompting
- Taste Skill is a large dialed rulebook and blocking pre-flight layer
- MIFB is micro-detail craft
- Impeccable is toolchain enforcement
- UI UX Pro Max is retrieval
- Vercel is runtime fetch-and-audit
- gpt-tasteskill can drift and should not be treated as an automatic superset.
- This note is part of the S5h legacy migration and is intentionally scoped to its assigned file.
- The evidence basis is limited to the assigned claim packs, raw captures, and source-ledger URLs.
- Counts, versions, stars, installs, release states, and dates are stated as dated observations.
- Same-author pages are treated as consistency checks unless an independent source is named.
- The note keeps wiki links pathless where a canonical title exists.
- The note avoids source claims that were not present in the local evidence pack.
