# Dials Hub

Taste Skill parameterizes aesthetics into three integer dials (each 1-10), set **conversationally per project, never by editing the file**. Baseline **8 / 6 / 4**.

- [[The Three Dials]] - the model and why it works
- [[DESIGN_VARIANCE]] - layout, centered to experimental
- [[MOTION_INTENSITY]] - animation depth, static to cinematic
- [[VISUAL_DENSITY]] - information per viewport, airy to cockpit
- [[Dial Tuning Guide]] - inference table + use-case presets
