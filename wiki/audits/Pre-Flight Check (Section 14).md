Pre-Flight Check (Section 14) is the mandatory Taste Skill v2 final gate: every box must pass, while the popular 60-item count remains contested.

## What it is
- Raw SKILL.md states Section 14 is not optional
- Any failed checkbox means output is not done
- Official guide requires every box marked Pass or Fail with one-line justification
- neodrop calls the check a 60-item preflight check
- Official site and raw SKILL.md do not state a 60-item count in captured evidence
- The count claim stays contested
- The check re-verifies design read, dials, design system, redesign mode, dash ban, locks, contrast, hero, layout, assets, motion, performance, and scope.
- This note is part of the S5h legacy migration and is intentionally scoped to its assigned file.
- The evidence basis is limited to the assigned claim packs, raw captures, and source-ledger URLs.
- Counts, versions, stars, installs, release states, and dates are stated as dated observations.
- Same-author pages are treated as consistency checks unless an independent source is named.
- The note keeps wiki links pathless where a canonical title exists.
- The note avoids source claims that were not present in the local evidence pack.
