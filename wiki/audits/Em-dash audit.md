# Em-dash audit

Confidence tag: EVIDENCE-BASED.

## Compiled Truth

Run in **both** workflows. Scans all visible text for the em-dash `—` (U+2014) and en-dash `–` (U+2013). Result must be **zero** anywhere - headlines, eyebrows, labels, pills, buttons, captions, nav, body, quote attribution. Ranges use the plain hyphen `-`. A single occurrence fails the [[Pre-Flight Check (Section 14)]]. Full rule: [[Em-Dash Ban]].
