# Section-layout-repetition audit

Confidence tag: EVIDENCE-BASED.

## Compiled Truth

Run in the **greenfield** workflow. In writing, **list each section's layout family**. Requirement: each family appears at most once, and an 8-section page uses **>= 4 different layout families**. Also catches the zigzag cap (max 2 consecutive image+text splits). Full rule: [[Layout and Section Rules]].
