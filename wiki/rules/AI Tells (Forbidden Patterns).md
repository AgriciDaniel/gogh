# AI Tells (Forbidden Patterns)

Confidence tag: EVIDENCE-BASED (canonical `SKILL.md` §9; `tasteskill.dev` Quick Reminders).

## Compiled Truth

§9 is the ban list - the concrete "tells" that mark a page as AI-generated. It is the negative image of [[AI Slop]], turned into machine-checkable rules.

**§9.A - Visual:** no neon glows, no pure black, no oversaturated accents, no custom cursors.

**§9.C - Layout:** no 3-column equal feature cards.

**§9.D - "Jane Doe" content:** no generic names/avatars; no fake-perfect numbers (`99.99%`, `50%`); no startup-slop names ("Acme", "Nexus", "SmartFlow"); no filler verbs ("Elevate", "Seamless", "Unleash", "Next-Gen", "Revolutionize").

**§9.F - Production-Test Tells (hard bans):**
- Hero version labels (`V0.6`, `BETA`, `INVITE-ONLY`); "Brand No. 01" sub-eyebrows.
- **Section-number eyebrows** (`00 / INDEX`, `001 Capabilities`, `06 how it works`); `01 / 4` pagination; numbered scroll cues.
- Middle-dot `·` rationed to max 1 per line; decorative colored status dots (zero by default).
- `<br>`-broken italic headlines; vertical rotated text; crosshair/hairline decoration.
- Div-based fake product UI; fake version footers; "Quietly in use at" / "Quietly trusted by".
- Poetic labels ("From the field", "Field notes", "On our desks"); weather/locale/city/time strips (banned "for 99% of briefs").
- Micro-meta sentences under eyebrows; generic step labels ("Stage 1/2/3", "Phase 01/02/03").
- Pills/labels overlaid on images; photo-credit captions as decoration.
- Version footers (`v1.4.2`, `Build 0048`) on marketing pages; live-stock counters.
- Hero-bottom decoration strips (`BRAND. MOTION. SPATIAL.`); floating top-right sub-text.
- `border-t` + `border-b` on every list row; scoring bars with filled tracks.
- Scroll cues (`Scroll`, `↓ scroll`, `Scroll to explore`).

**§9.G - The [[Em-Dash Ban]]:** zero `—`/`–` anywhere. Its own note.

**§0.D - Anti-default discipline:** do not default to AI-purple gradients, centered hero over dark mesh, three equal feature cards, generic glassmorphism, infinite-loop micro-animations, or Inter + slate-900.
