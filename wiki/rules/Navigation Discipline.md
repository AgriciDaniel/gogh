# Navigation Discipline

Confidence tag: EVIDENCE-BASED (canonical `SKILL.md` §4.7; `tasteskill.dev` Quick Reminders).

## Compiled Truth

- **Navigation must render on a single line at desktop.**
- **Height cap: 80px max**, default **64-72px**.

That is the whole rule, and it is a mechanical pre-flight item. It exists to stop the common slop pattern of a tall, wrapping, over-stuffed nav that pushes the hero down and competes with content. Pattern vocabulary for richer navs (Dock Magnification, Magnetic Button, Dynamic Island, Mega Menu) lives in §10 - see [[Layout and Section Rules]] - but all of them still obey the single-line, <=80px cap.
